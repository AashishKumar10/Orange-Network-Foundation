Our Journey

Key milestones in building Brand Bharat through innovation and cultural preservation.

Foundation & Vision

Orange Network Foundation registered as Trust in New Delhi. Core focus on establishing organizational structure, leadership team, and foundational vision for building Brand Bharat through comprehensive development initiatives.

VGI Framework Development

Vedic General Intelligence conceptualized and core architecture developed - India's first culturally-rooted AI combining 25,000 years of Vedic wisdom with cutting-edge artificial intelligence technology.

ONF Scanner Launch (Guru Purnima)

World's first philosophy-driven, open-source cultural heritage digitization platform launched on the auspicious day of Guru Purnima - democratizing AI for cultural preservation worldwide.

Digital Heritage Mission

Launch ambitious mission to digitize 1 Million books and manuscripts, creating the world's largest Vedic and cultural knowledge
repository with advanced AI-powered
search and analysis.

VGI Ecosystem Deployment

Complete VGI system launch with specialized AI models designed to outperform 100B+ parameter LLMs in Vedic domains, Sanskrit processing, and cultural context
understanding.

AI Product Suite

Launch comprehensive ecosystem of 10+ AI Made in India open-source products including ONF Translator, ONF Writer, specialized SLMs, and culturally-intelligent applications.

National Partnerships

Establish strategic partnerships with Indian universities, government bodies, and cultural institutions for nationwide digital asset preservation and AI-driven cultural
renaissance.

Global Cultural Leadership

Position Bharat as the global leader in culturally-embedded AI, sustainable digital innovation, and ethical technology development, inspiring worldwide cultural preservation movements.
